# Contributor Guide

Thank you for your interest in improving PyCALI. PyCALI is open-source under the MIT License and welcomes contributions in the form of bug reports, feature requests, and pull requests.

If you want to join in the developing team, feel free to reach out. 

## How to report a bug?

Report bugs on the [Issue Tracker](https://github.com/LiyrAstroph/PyCALI/issues).

## How to request a feature

Feel free to request features on the [Issue
Tracker](https://github.com/LiyrAstroph/PyCALI/issues).

## How to submit changes

Open a [Pull Request](https://github.com/LiyrAstroph/PyCALI/pulls).
